This is a gentle reminder to rest your eyes just for a while.

According to Healthline, a famous health magazine, the 20-20-20 rule is an effective means of countering eye train caused by exposure to screens. Basically every 20 minutes, you look at an object 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This helps relax the muscles of the eye that work so hard to make your eye focus on the screen.
